Welcome! I am a researcher in the area of wireless communications. I have a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech with a focus on wireless communication systems. I work primarily in PHY/MAC/RF layers of wireless communications. I am proficient in theoretical modeling, analysis, and simulation of wireless systems. I have designed and carried out experimental studies using RF and microwave tools and devices.

Currently, I am working on next generation wireless system design/architecture, technology and standards development at Samsung Research America. I am a delegate to the IEEE 802.11 and 802.15 standards, and also contribute to 3GPP RAN1 TDocs on Integrated Access and Backhaul. My research interests include coexistence in wireless systems, mmWave communications, dynamic spectrum access, and machine learning applied to wireless communications.
